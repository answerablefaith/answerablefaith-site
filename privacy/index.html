Privacy Policy

How Answerable Faith handles information across the app and website.

Effective date: 29 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Answerable Faith handles information when you use our app or website.

Who We Are

Answerable Faith is provided by answerablefaith.com.

Contact email: support@answerablefaith.com

Information We Collect

Answerable Faith does not require users to create an account and does not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, address, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, or files from your device.

The app may store progress, preferences, completed activities, saved content, or settings locally on your device. This information is used to make the app work properly and improve your experience. Local app data is not uploaded to our servers by us.

If you contact us for support, we will receive the information you choose to send, such as your email address, message content, and any screenshots or details you include.

Purchases and Subscriptions

If the app offers paid features, purchases, or subscriptions, payments are processed by the relevant app store, such as the Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

The app may use app store services to check purchase status and unlock paid features.

App Updates and Technical Checks

The app may check whether a newer version is available or whether app content needs to be updated. This is used to maintain the app and provide current functionality.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website is hosted using GitHub Pages and is intended to operate as a simple static website. We do not intentionally use cookies, advertising trackers, analytics cookies, or similar tracking technologies on this website.

If this changes in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy and, where required, ask for consent before using non-essential cookies or similar technologies.

Information We Do Not Collect

Unless this changes and this policy is updated, Answerable Faith does not intentionally collect:

  • precise location data;
  • advertising identifiers for tracking;
  • contacts;
  • photos or files from your device;
  • health or financial information;
  • personal data for sale to third parties.

How We Use Information

We use information only where needed to:

  • operate and maintain the app;
  • save your progress and preferences on your device;
  • verify purchases or subscriptions where applicable;
  • respond to support requests;
  • fix problems and improve the app;
  • comply with legal obligations where required.

Legal Basis for Processing

Where data protection law requires a legal basis for processing, we rely on appropriate legal bases, including contract, legitimate interests, legal obligation, and consent where required.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information only where necessary with service providers that help operate the app, app stores that process purchases, or where required by law.

International Use

Answerable Faith may be used by people in different countries. If you contact us or use services connected to the app, information may be processed in the United Kingdom or other countries where our service providers operate. We take reasonable steps to handle information in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable law.

Data Retention

Local app data remains on your device unless you delete it or delete the app. Support emails may be kept for as long as reasonably needed to respond to your request, maintain records, resolve issues, and comply with legal obligations.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, including if you are in the United Kingdom, European Economic Area, California, or another region with privacy rights, you may have rights over personal information we hold about you.

These rights may include the right to request access to, correction of, deletion of, or restriction of personal information. You may also have the right to object to certain processing or to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To make a request, contact: support@answerablefaith.com

Children's Privacy

Answerable Faith is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can review and address the issue.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information we handle. However, no app, website, or email system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Complaints

If you are unhappy with how we handle your personal information, please contact us first so we can try to resolve the issue.

If you are in the UK, you also have the right to complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office: https://ico.org.uk/make-a-complaint/

If you are outside the UK, you may also have the right to complain to your local data protection authority or privacy regulator.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
