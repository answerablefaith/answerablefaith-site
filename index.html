How the training works

Practise the flow you want in real conversations: face a real question, answer before peeking, then review with Scripture, context and a model response.

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers for Christians, churches and groups deciding whether Answerable Faith is for them.

What is Answerable Faith?

A Christian apologetics training app for practising thoughtful, Scripture-led answers to real questions.

Who is it for?

Regular Christians, evangelical groups, small groups, youth and student ministries, and churches that want to answer with confidence and grace.

How do conversation partners work?

Partners represent different beliefs, temperaments and objections so you can practise realistic conversations before they happen.
