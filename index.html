Frequently asked questions

What is Answerable Faith?

Answerable Faith is a Christian apologetics trainer designed to help you practise thoughtful answers to real questions about faith, Scripture, and reason.

Who is it for?

It is for Christians who want to grow in confidence, clarity, and grace when talking with friends, family, students, or seekers.

How do conversation partners work?

Conversation partners represent different viewpoints and objections so you can practise responding with patience before real conversations happen.

How can I contact you?

You can reach us through the contact page or email support@answerablefaith.com.
