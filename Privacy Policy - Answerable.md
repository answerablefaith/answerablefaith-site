# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 29 June 2026

## Privacy Policy

Answerable is provided by answerablefaith.com and is designed to work primarily offline.

### Information We Collect

Your progress, streaks, completed challenges, settings and preferences are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.

If you purchase Answerable Premium, the App communicates with Apple's services to verify your purchase and unlock premium features. We do not receive or store your payment information.

When the App is opened, it may check whether a newer version of the App is available. This check is used only to decide whether to show an update message inside the App. We do not use this feature to create user profiles or track your activity.

If you contact us for support, we will receive the information you provide in your email, including your email address and the contents of your message.

### How We Use Information

We use information only to:

- Save your progress on your device.
- Verify Premium purchases.
- Check whether a newer version of the App is available and, if appropriate, show an update message.
- Respond to support requests.

We do not sell, rent or share your personal information for advertising purposes.

### Data Storage

Your progress and settings are stored locally on your device. You can remove this data by deleting the App from your device, unless it has been backed up by your device.

### Data Retention

We do not retain personal information unless you choose to contact us for support.

### Third-Party Services

The App uses Apple services to verify Premium purchases and may connect to our servers to check for available app updates.

### Your Rights

If you are located in the United Kingdom or European Economic Area, you have rights under applicable data protection laws, including the right to access, correct or request deletion of any personal information we hold about you.
